# FAQs

### 1. What is Karnataka State Police Datathon 2026?
Datathon 2026 is a nationwide innovation challenge hosted by the **Karnataka State Police**. It aims to leverage the power of data to build smarter, safer, and more efficient systems for law enforcement and public safety.

---

### 2. Who is eligible to participate?
The Datathon is open to a wide range of innovators, including **students, working professionals, startups, and independent developers**.

---

### 3. What are the problem statements we need to solve?
- **Challenge 1:** Intelligent Conversational AI for the KSP Crime Database.
- **Challenge 2:** AI-Driven Crime Analytics & Visualization Platform.

---

### 4. What is the total prize pool for the Datathon?
There is a massive **total prize pool of ₹10 Lakhs**, allocated across both challenges to recognize impactful solutions.

---

### 5. How is the prize money distributed per challenge?
For each challenge, the structure is:
- **Winner:** ₹2.5 L
- **1st Runner-up:** ₹1.5 L
- **2nd Runner-up:** ₹1.0 L

---

### 6. Why should I participate in this hackathon?
Build solutions with **true public purpose**, showcase your skills to **government stakeholders**, and network with industry experts while solving real-world crime data challenges.
